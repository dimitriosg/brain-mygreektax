## Flagged: negative margin

**AFM registration.** Retail from EUR 39. Chrysostomos wholesale for AFM + Setup (AFM, κλειδάριθμος, TAXISnet and myAADE activation, the version almost every relocating client needs) is EUR 45. At current retail this service runs at a EUR 6 loss per case with Chrysostomos before any of your own time or overhead. The AFM Basic line (AFM number only, e.g. remote property buyers) is EUR 30 wholesale, which does clear a margin, but that is not the typical client. This needs a decision: raise retail on the Setup version, or accept it as pure acquisition cost and price the loss in deliberately.

**Note on the "from EUR 299 / EUR 199" retail figures.** The current site pricing appears to quote one flat "from" figure per direction (to Greece / from Greece) rather than by the three-tier complexity split Annex A actually prices against (simple / medium / complex). Since wholesale ranges from EUR 50 to EUR 100 depending on complexity, a single flat retail figure either underprices complex cases or overprices simple ones. Worth considering three retail tiers that mirror the wholesale structure, the margin swing between simple and complex is too large to leave flat.

## Margin principles

- Tier 1 is acquisition, not profit, thin or negative margin is accepted by design, but the AFM negative margin above should be a deliberate choice, not an accident.
- Real margin lives in Tier 4: residency transfers, 5A, complex and back-year cases.
- Revisit any service where wholesale exceeds roughly 60% of retail. On the confirmed Chrysostomos figures, that currently flags: AFM registration (Setup version, already flagged above) and E1 with extras if retail stays at EUR 49.
