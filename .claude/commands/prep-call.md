Prepare Δημήτρης for a call. Input: $ARGUMENTS (who, and the purpose of the call). Paste of the recent thread is welcome; if the person is a client, their live data comes from the paste, never from the brain.

Follow CLAUDE.md rules strictly. Build the brief from: the pasted context, matching case patterns in `cases/`, rules and SOPs in `wiki/`, and for partner calls the wholesale side of `pricing/` only.

Output exactly one page with these sections:

**Context.** Two or three sentences: who this is, where the relationship stands, what triggered the call.

**Their situation.** The facts that matter, in order of importance. Flag anything unverified as unverified.

**Open items.** What is unresolved, who owes what.

**Likely questions and answers.** The three to six questions they will probably ask, each with a short sourced answer. Where the honest answer is "the partner must confirm," say exactly that and phrase the holding line Δημήτρης can use on the call.

**Questions to ask them.** What Δημήτρης needs to extract from this call, ordered.

**For partner calls only: negotiation notes.** Wholesale target per service, walk-away point, what to concede and what not to. Retail prices, margins, and markup logic never appear here and are never spoken on the call (rule R2). Include the two mandatory checks for new candidates: active έναρξη επιτηδεύματος (hard blocker if missing) and comfort with the εξουσιοδότηση-only access workflow.

**Desired outcome and fallback.** One line each.
