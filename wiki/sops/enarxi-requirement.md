# Partner requirement: active έναρξη επιτηδεύματος

Status: CANONICAL (internal policy)
Last reviewed: 2026-07-12
Affects: partner onboarding, all partner payments

## The rule
Every accountant partner must have an active έναρξη επιτηδεύματος with AADE. This is a hard blocker: without it the partner cannot legally issue τιμολόγια to MyGreekTax and cannot be paid by the entity. No έναρξη means no onboarding, regardless of how strong the candidate is otherwise.

## How to verify
Ask directly on the qualification call: "Έχετε κάνει έναρξη επιτηδεύματος; Θα χρειαστεί να εκδίδετε τιμολόγιο προς εμάς για κάθε υπηρεσία που εκτελείτε."

## Payment flow
All invoicing and payment flows through the legal entity (Xolo Go OÜ, Estonian, EU VAT registered). VAT is handled automatically by Xolo based on counterparty location.
