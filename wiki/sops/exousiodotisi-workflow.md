# Client access: εξουσιοδότηση only, never credentials

Status: CANONICAL (internal policy)
Last reviewed: 2026-07-12
Affects: every filing, every partner, every client

## The rule
Client TAXISnet and myAADE credentials are NEVER shared with anyone: not with partners, not stored in this brain, not transmitted by email. All partner access happens through formal εξουσιοδότηση from the client to the accountant, scoped to the specific filing.

## How it works per service
- E1 tax returns: εξουσιοδότηση via myAADE for the specific declaration
- AFM registration and other filings: the equivalent scoped authorization for that process
- The authorization names the partner, covers only what the filing requires, and ends with it

## Why this framing matters
It protects the client's broader TAXISnet account and is a core trust point. Communicate it proactively to clients (safety) and confirm comfort with it on every partner qualification call: "Στη συνεργασία μας, δεν θα σας δίνουμε ποτέ τα credentials TAXISnet των πελατών. Η πρόσβαση θα γίνεται αποκλειστικά μέσω εξουσιοδότησης από τον πελάτη προς εσάς, scoped στη συγκεκριμένη δήλωση."
